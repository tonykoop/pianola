# Image Generation Prompts

Concept visualization only; not fabrication authority.

## Hero Prompt

Photorealistic studio product photo of a pianola-style player piano mechanism presented as a finished review prototype: a polished upright piano front with an exposed perforated paper roll, tracker bar, neat rows of small pneumatic tubes, removable pouch-and-valve modules, and a pneumatic action interface leading toward the piano keys. Show the roll transport, take-up spool, tracker-bar sensing face, and serviceable valve chest clearly, with warm wood casework, brass fittings, cream paper roll, and black felt or leather pneumatic parts. Clean studio background, soft directional lighting, 3/4 product-photography angle, approximately 50mm lens, high detail, photorealistic.

## In-Context Prompt

Photorealistic workshop testing scene of the pianola mechanism with a short perforated test strip moving across one tracker-bar port, a technician hand-cranking or adjusting the roll transport while the exposed pouch and pilot valve module sits open for inspection. Include a piano action test rail, small leak-check tubing, and service tools on a bench, natural daylight, careful prototype atmosphere, no decorative brand logos or fake tune names.

## Detail Prompt

Photorealistic macro close-up of the tracker bar and pneumatic chain: a perforated paper roll crossing a smooth tracker-bar port, tiny tubes leading to a flexible pouch and valve seat, and a small pneumatic actuator linkage in the background. Show clean sealing surfaces, visible service screws, paper texture, and soft leather/felt materials. Shallow depth of field, high detail, realistic mechanical scale.

## Negative / Avoid

Avoid text, watermarks, logos, impossible paper paths, random piano keys moving without action linkage, extra limbs, distorted hands, steam-punk clutter, and fake measured hole spacing.
